Information


La Lumière is a small, independent jewellery business that creates hand-made polymer clay charms, necklaces and other items. The inspiration behind the brand's name lies in the French essence of simple elegance, as well as ‘the light’ which is created from the pleasures of creativity. Polymer clay is an art medium that is known for its versatility, pliability and simplicity to work with. It is an oven bake modelling material composed of polymers, resins, colouring agents and fillers.
